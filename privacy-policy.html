Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Bullard Locks ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website, contact our services, or engage with our locksmith services in North London.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Address Information: Property address for service delivery
  • Service Details: Description of locksmith services required
  • Communication Records: Details of our conversations and correspondence

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral sources
  • Cookies: Information about your browsing preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide locksmith services and respond to emergencies
  • Communication: To contact you about your service requests and updates
  • Customer Service: To handle inquiries, complaints, and feedback
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Business Improvement: To improve our services and customer experience

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK data protection law (including GDPR), we process your personal information based on:

  • Contract: To perform our locksmith services contract with you
  • Legitimate Interest: To communicate with you and improve our services
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations
  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent for specific processing

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us deliver services (e.g., payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Emergency Situations: To protect the safety of individuals or property
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a business sale or merger

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Secure transmission and storage of data
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Security systems are regularly updated and maintained
  • Physical Security: Secure storage of paper records and devices

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our locksmith services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records for accounting purposes

Typically, we retain customer records for 7 years after the last service, in line with UK accounting requirements.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
